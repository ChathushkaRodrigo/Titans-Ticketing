How to Get Help

If you need help with Titans Ticketing, please contact the app operator or support team responsible for your event deployment.

Recommended Support Contact Details

Before publishing this page, replace the placeholders below with your real support information:

  • Email: corsdeck@gmail.com
  • Company or team: Titans Ticketing Support
  • Response target: Within 2 to 3 business days

Include the Following in Your Request

  • Your name and organization
  • The device type and iOS version
  • The app version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Relevant ticket ID, batch ID, or participant reference where applicable

Common Support Topics

  • Login and account access issues
  • Ticket creation or update problems
  • QR scanning and check-in issues
  • Reservation, approval, or rejection workflow issues
  • Data correction or privacy-related requests

Security and Privacy Requests

For requests involving personal data, correction, deletion, or privacy concerns, please submit your request through the designated support email and include enough detail for verification and response.

Service Availability Note

Titans Ticketing depends on active backend, database, and infrastructure services. Temporary issues may occur during maintenance windows, deployments, or upstream provider incidents.
