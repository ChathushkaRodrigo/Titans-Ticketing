Security Overview

  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS while the app communicates with backend services.
  • Application data is stored in MongoDB with encryption protections configured for the deployed environment.
  • Access to operational data is restricted to authorized admins and service workflows.
  • Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect customer information.
